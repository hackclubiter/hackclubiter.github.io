Rules & Regulations

At Hack Club ITER, we have certain rules and regulations that the Core Members must follow. If they failed to do so, then he/she will be expelled from the core team as well as from the community. These rules and regulations are strict.

  • Applicant must be a current student of ITER.
    • Must be a student of the Institute of Technical Education & Research, SOA University.
    • Must be a resident of India.
    • Should have a minimum CGPA of 5.0.
    • Must not be a leader/coordinator of any other club/community.
    • Can devote 7 hours per week of his/her time for club's work.
  • Applicants must have a proper CV as it will be sent to the Hack Club HQ to be updated in their database.
  • Applicant must have a GitHub account.
  • Applicant must not be a lead/coordinator of any other technical clubs in ITER.
  • Applicant must have joined the Telegram group of Hack Club ITER.
  • The applicant has to actively participate in all the events organized by the community and must contribute actively to the further growth of the community.
  • If the Leads don't think that a core member is living up to their expectations, then they can dismiss him/her as a core member whenever they want.
  • Any core member can also contribute to other roles if he/she is interested, but he/she can do so only after efficiently performing the role for which they were specifically chosen.

Although the rules are a bit strict but don't worry there will be some relaxation depending upon the situation. So don't get overwhelmed by the rules. Our only prerequisite is that a core member should be active in the community, active in the sense that he/she has to take initiative, not that he/she will do work if and only when they are assigned some.
